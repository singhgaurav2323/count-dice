Game Rules

×
  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wish. Each result get added to his Round tally.
  • But, if the player rolls a '1', all his Round score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his Round score gets added to his Global score.
  • The first player to reach targetted GLOBAL score or can change the score to wins the game.

* can change target score in Final Score
